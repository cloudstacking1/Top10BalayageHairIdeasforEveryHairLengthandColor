What is Balayage?

Derived from the French word meaning "to sweep," balayage is a freehand hair coloring technique that creates a natural, sun-kissed look. Unlike traditional highlights that use foils, balayage involves painting color directly onto the hair, resulting in a softer, more blended appearance. This method offers a low-maintenance solution, as it grows out gracefully without harsh lines.
